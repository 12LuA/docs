### FAQ
<Accordions>
  <Accordion id='what-happens' title="What happens when I am ratelimited?">
    When you are rate-limited, you will receive a response like the one below, you can use this to wait until your ratelimit has been lifted before sending another response.

  ```json
    {
      "code": 429,
      "message": "You are being rate limited limit, Try again soon.",
      // Seconds till the rate limit expires
      "expiresIn": 26
    }
  ```
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='higher-limits' title="Would you like higher rate limits?">
    For any requests regarding ratelimits, please reach out to our development team in [Our Discord](https://discord.com/invite/jjEauJXuZc).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion id='perm-banned' title="I am permanently banned from the API, what can I do?">
    If you've been permanently banned from using our API, you can reach out to our team in [Our Discord](https://discord.com/invite/jjEauJXuZc).


    ```json
    {
      "code": 403,
      "message": "You have been permanently banned from using our API, please contact our team to appeal.",
    }
    ```
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
